How can you earn more money as a game developer?

Development opportunities funnel into only a few specific silos where power is concentrated. This leaves a massive opportunity gap separating indie developers from those signed with big publishers. And with the amount of money those publishers are making and able to spend, that gap is only going to widen, especially when considering marketing and distribution costs. Ultimately, this leaves independent developers disenfranchised and even unable to bring their ideas to market.

Here are a few things Game Developers can do with Apstract in order to increase revenue, engaging users and improving the overall gaming experience at the same time.

  • Sell your game items directly to the players, keeping 90% of the revenue unlike the 70% industry standard.
  • Crowdfund your game by selling in-game assets before launch.
  • Enable open secondary markets for trading, selling, and reselling of in-game assets, and capture 5-10% of all transactions.
  • Take data driven decisions - get access to all your data and top level analytics

Incentive based referral marketing - Incentivize streamers, influencers and all players to market and resell your game assets.
