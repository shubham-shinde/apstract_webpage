What is blockchain technology and how can it empower players?

Games are usually where a new technology comes to incubate. From advances in real time graphics, to AR, VR and Cloud Computing, games have always been the early adopters of new technologies. Blockchain tech also comes up with such promise, of disrupting the entire gaming industry by completely changing the way gamers interact with their in-game assets.

Blockchain is a peer to peer distributed ledger, where digital information can be recorded and distributed, and is accessible to everybody. Essentially, it is an immutable, decentralized, secure and transparent medium of storing and distributing digital information.

Blockchain enables creation of a new standard for digital assets, where a digital asset can be tied to a unique digital token and stored on the Blockchain. These unique digital tokens are called Non Fungible Tokens (NFTs).

Game Items when tied to unique digital tokens, can exhibit following exemplary properties

  • Authentic Ownership - Players can truly own their in-game items, where no one can alter, replicate, destroy or take them away.
  • Verifiable Scarcity - Players can verify if their assets are as rare as the developer claims them to be.
  • Transparency - Players can have complete transparency into the in-game economies
  • Liquidity - Players can have the opportunity to trade, sell, gift or rent their in-game items with complete autonomy.

Blockchain provides a wide avenue of possibilities for game developers to provide great control to players, and in-turn alleviate their overall gaming experience.
